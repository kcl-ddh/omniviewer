jQuery OmniViewer

OmniViewer is a jQuery plugin developed by Matteo Romanello at the CCH (Centre for Computing in the Humanities) Department of Digital Humanities, King's College London.

Getting Started

This plugin comes in the following flavours:

  • Zoomify Flavour (working)
  • IIP Flavour (broken)
  • Djatoka Flavour (broken)
